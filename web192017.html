The Ho Chi Minh City Museum preserves many valuable historical and cultural artifacts, reflecting the formation and development of ancient Saigon - Gia Dinh. The artifacts include ancient coins, costumes, household items, weapons, and documentary images from the French colonial period to the present. Some outstanding artifacts such as ancient maps, typewriters, film cameras, revolutionary items, etc. are all displayed in a solemn ancient French architectural space. These artifacts contribute to clearly recreating the life and spirit of the city through historical periods.
