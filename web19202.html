The Vietnam Literature Museum is a place to preserve, display and introduce national literature products from traditional to modern. The center of the club in Hanoi, the museum gives visitors a comprehensive view of the development of Vietnamese literature through images, artifacts, autographs, works and spaces that recreate the creative lives of famous writers and poets. This is not only a place for those who love literature programs but also a place to educate, inspire and preserve the spiritual cultural values ​​of the nation.
